Help / Support

Find answers to common questions below or reach out to our support team directly.

How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to the Wallet page, select the Withdraw tab, choose your payment method, enter your account number and amount, then submit. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
Why is my balance negative after Round 3?
Round 3 features Premium Brands. Rating a premium brand requires a top-up to unlock multiplied earnings. Contact support for assistance.
How do I deposit funds?
Go to Wallet, select Deposit, choose your method, enter an amount, and contact customer care via WhatsApp or Telegram to complete the deposit.
