About Global Brands

The world's premier brand rating and discovery platform.

Our Mission
Global Brands exists to give ordinary people a voice in rating the world's most influential companies. From Apple to Zara, every iconic brand has shaped how we live, work, and communicate. We believe your opinion matters and we reward you for sharing it. Our platform connects brand enthusiasts across Africa and beyond with 96 of the most recognised brands in human history.
How We Pay You
We pay real rewards directly to your mobile wallet. Kenyan users receive payments via M-Pesa. Ugandan users receive via MTN MoMo or Airtel Money. Tanzanian users receive via M-Pesa. Nigerian users receive via bank transfer. All other countries receive via wallet address. Minimum withdrawal is the equivalent of KES 10 and maximum is KES 250,000 per day. Customer support is available 24 hours a day via WhatsApp and Telegram.
